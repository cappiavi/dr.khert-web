01 · About Me

Compassion meets innovation

A future physician passionate about pediatric healthcare, medical research, and the responsible use of technology to make care more accessible.

I am an aspiring medical doctor pursuing a specialization in Pediatrics, driven by a deep commitment to child healthcare and preventive medicine. My goal is to deliver care that treats not only illness but nurtures the wellbeing of every child and family I serve.

Alongside medicine, I bring a strong background in Information and Communication Technology. My experience spans programming, cybersecurity, computer networking, and artificial intelligence — a foundation I intend to use to bridge the gap between healthcare and modern technology.

I am fascinated by medical research and improving patient outcomes, and I believe the future of medicine lies in combining clinical excellence with intelligent, secure, and accessible digital tools.
